Features

FEATURES

Chatrooms

Includes fully interactive chatrooms with custom emotes, image attachments and more!

Accounts

Update your profile with Twitch, Steam and Discord links so friends know where to find you.

Connect

Meet gamers with similar game interests chat about the latest gaming news.

Mobile accessibility

The application is usable on all mobile devices and offered free-of-charge.

Open-Source

This project is completely open-source with documentation available on github here.

Email

Accounts verification and recovery are available via email to prevent user identity theft.

Chatroom

  • Chatrooms for each of the most popular video games.
  • Search user profiles to connect with them on your favorite gaming platform.
  • Ability to add emojis and image attachments to messages.
  • Create your own custom emotes to use in chat just like Twitch or Discord, except for free!
  • Ability to post anonymously if you do not wish to create an account.

Games library

  • Shows the most exciting upcoming, recently released and popular games, as well as gaming news articles.
  • Includes Steam game prices, if available.
  • Adanced search with filtering and sorting by popularity, category, genre and platform.
  • Easy-to-use swipe navigation.

Profiles

Connect with other gamer's profiles to view their Twitch, Steam and Discord links to talk and play with them outside the app.

Twitch and Steam integration

  • View your followed stream's viewer count, tier, cheer and badge emotes.
  • Watch your followed Twitch streams with optional chat or video.
  • View your Steam friends, their online status and the game they're currently playing.
